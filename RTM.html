About Our Music

Ron & Trish perform musically for different events and they have over 35 years of experience in bringing their love for music to events for other people. Ron plays saxophone and guitar, Trish plays keyboard, and they both sing. The Duo, also offers a Comedy-Music Show. They are available, for dances, private parties, fundraisers, fairs, festivals.
Ron and Trish are known for their wit and banter with the audience, and love the constant recommendations and amazing reviews received every time they perform.
